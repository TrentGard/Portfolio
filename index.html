About Me.A Hoosier Native Turned Texan

Hello, I'm Trent!

I attended the Indiana University Kelly School of Business, during which time I was fortunate enough to study abroad in both Cuernavaca, Mexico, and Barcelona, Spain.

Side projects during these times included teaching English to local students in Mexico and doing online marketing during my internship in Spain.

I have extensive experience in retail marketing and management as evidenced in my work history.

I moved to Austin, Texas, to continue scratching my exploratory itch as well as to seek new challenges and opportunities.

My hobbies include writing music, ping pong, golfing, bowling, and cooking.
