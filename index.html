About Me.A Hoosier Native Turned Texan

Hello, I'm Trent!

I attended the Indiana University Kelly School of Business, during which time I was fortunate enough to study abroad in both Cuernavaca, Mexico, and Barcelona, Spain.

I moved to Austin, Texas, to continue scratching my exploratory itch as well as to seek new challenges and opportunities.

My hobbies include coding, writing music, ping pong, golf, bowling, and cooking.
